ENTERPRISE RESOURCE PLANNING

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is a software system that integrates all the business processes and data of an organization into a single, unified system. It is a comprehensive solution that helps businesses manage their operations, resources, and customers.

SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

Supply Chain Management (SCM) is a software system that integrates all the supply chain processes and data of an organization into a single, unified system. It is a comprehensive solution that helps businesses manage their supply chain, from the purchase of raw materials to the delivery of finished products to customers.

CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a software system that integrates all the customer processes and data of an organization into a single, unified system. It is a comprehensive solution that helps businesses manage their customers, from the initial contact to the post-sale follow-up.

For business processes to be integrated, the data from the different systems must be unified. This is where ERP comes in.
